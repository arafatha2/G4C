Bite Right

Arafath Ahmed (HS Junior)

Game Designing Learner

PITCH

Ignore the junk, accept the veggies, and level up in a food decision game named Bite Right! Choose between healthy food or junk food and progress based on your decisions. Bright visuals and fun gameplay promote healthy eating habits. Will you “Bite Right”?

THEME

Bite Right fits the “Nurture Yourself With Nature” theme by encouraging better eating habits. Choose wisely in a swipe-based game: right for healthy, left for junk. Score points and track progress through an engaging profile system.

MY WHY

I created Bite Right because I used to struggle with unhealthy food choices. That led to dental surgeries and other risks. Through this game, I want to help others make smarter food decisions while having fun!

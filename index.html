Bite Right

Arafath Ahmed (HS Junior)

Game Designing Learner

PITCH

Ignore the junk, accept the veggies, and level up in a food decision game named Bite Right!. The players will have a choice between healthy food or junk food. Depending on their choice, they will either progress or fail. What are YOU gonna choose? With the bright and beautiful colors, Bite Right will make you learn more about the healthy eating habits you should take into consideration. Food facts, quick rounds, and cool visuals while you play. This fun and engaging game won’t just be like any other game but a tasty challenge for your brain! Will you “Bite Right”?

THEME

In Bite Right, the player comes into a game where they make proper food choices to keep their health bar from depleting. There’s short reminders that talk about healthy food and how good they are when eaten. The vegetables will be bright to stand out while the junk food will look very dark. In the game, players will swipe to collect the food. When swiping for junk food, it causes the player to end the level. When swiping for healthy food, it increases the level. This allows the user to think of smart food choices that help their eating habits.

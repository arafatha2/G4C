PITCH

Ignore the junk, accept the veggies, and level up in a food decision game named Bite Right! Players will have a choice between healthy food or junk food. Depending on their choice, they will either progress or fail. What are YOU gonna choose? With bright and beautiful visuals, Bite Right helps you learn about healthy eating habits in a fun, fast-paced way. Food facts, quick rounds, and cool visuals make this more than just a game—it’s a tasty challenge for your brain! Will you “Bite Right”?

THEME

Bite Right fits the “Nurture Yourself With Nature” theme by encouraging better eating habits through a simple type of game play. In the game, you choose between healthy foods and junk foods to earn points. You can swipe right if the food is healthy and swipe left if the food is unhealthy. When you choose the right option, you are awarded with 100 points. If you choose the wrong however, you don't gain or lose any points. By doing this in the rounds given, at the end of the game where it displays your profile, it'll give your score!

MY WHY

I made this game because growing up, I’ve always loved eating unhealthy food. It got to a bad point where it messed up my oral health and caused me to have multiple teeth surgeries. I also had high risk of other diagnoses like diabetes and high cholesterol due to my family history. Because of that, I wanted to create a game that helps others make better choices too. I created Bite Right in hopes that people can learn about food in a fun way and start eating smarter.
